# About ActionX

ActionX is a commerce infrastructure layer built for modern AI products.

We help AI builders monetize conversational traffic by understanding purchase intent from natural language interactions and returning recommendation payloads that are ready to render in any UI.

## What We Believe

AI products are becoming the new front door for discovery and buying decisions.
As users ask AI assistants for suggestions, product comparisons, and purchase advice, monetization should feel native to the conversation, not forced by traditional ad slots.

That is the foundation of ActionX:

- AI-native monetization
- intent-aware recommendations
- flexible output for product teams
- integration-first architecture

## What ActionX Solves

Teams building AI chat, AI agents, and AI search tools often face the same challenge:

- high inference costs
- unclear monetization path
- limited control over recommendation quality and format

ActionX addresses this with API, SDK, and MCP-based integration paths that can be embedded into existing stacks.

## Who Uses ActionX

- AI chat products
- AI agent platforms
- AI search and recommendation experiences
- indie hackers and startups
- product teams building agentic commerce workflows

## Mission

Enable AI builders to create sustainable businesses by turning relevant user intent into transparent, high-quality commerce outcomes.
